Best Skateboards Buying Guide

Every skateboard mentioned above comes with its own set of features. So, it is always advisable to scrutinize the entire market before spending your hard-earned money on a skateboard. You must always pick one that suits your skillsets as well as budget.

If you are purchasing a skateboard for the first time, it can be an overwhelming experience. In this guide, we have shed light on some important factors that can help you make an informed decision and get your hands on the best skateboard.

What Factors Should You Consider Before Choosing a Skateboard?

Deck Size

Look for a skateboard with a deck width that is in proportion to the size of your shoe. If your shoe size ranges between 6.5 to 9 inches, the deck should be between 7.5 to 8 inches. The deck size also varies depending upon the surface you will be riding on

Generally, a deck of 8-inch wide and 30-inch long is considered suitable for most people. However, wider boards are perfect for beginners as they offer the best balance control and allow you to have a firm standing position.

Trucks

The size of the trucks depends on your deck size. If the length of the axle matches the deck width, it will ensure the trucks will fit the broadness of the board. Thus, the proper alignment will lead to the smooth sliding of the wheels.

Wheels

Wheels determine the speed limits of your mini cruiser. Typically, the best skateboard comes with mid-sized wheels that fall in the range of 52 mm to 54 mm are easier to be fixed on the trucks, eliminating the need for a riser pad. However, boards with wheels bigger than this range require a riser pad to sit beneath the trucks to offer swift turning.

Bearings

Bearings are the key to spin the wheels. The inner diameter of the bearings of a standard skateboard is 8mm. You should also consider the ABEC rating system of the bearings as it determines their tolerance as well as physical limit. Besides, you also have to keep the bearings clean and oiled to maintain them and extend their durability.

What Size of Skateboard is Best For You?

If you are purchasing a skateboard for the first time, you probably don’t know which size is perfect for you. Typically, skateboards are available in four different sizes such as:

Micro-Skateboard

This skateboard is suitable for kids aged around 5 years, having a shoe size of 3 or less. The width of these mini cruisers usually falls between 6.5 to 7 inches, while their length may range between 27 to 28 inches.

Mini-Skateboard

This skateboard is popular among children between 6 to 8 years old with a shoe size of 4 to 6. The width of this variant is seven inches approximately, while its length is usually around 28 inches.

Mid-Sized Skateboard

The mid-sized skateboards are ideal for individuals between the age of 9 to 12 years old with a shoe size of 7 to 8. Their width is usually around 7.5 inches, while their length can be nearly 28 inches.

Full-Sized Skateboard

These skateboards are preferred by teenagers with a shoe size of 9 or more. The width of this sports equipment usually lies between 7.5 to 8 inches, while their length ranges between 29 to 32 inches.

Final Words

Skateboarding is not a sport that you can learn easily. You have to invest a lot of time and effort to get adept at basic skills like balancing and some tricks.

Getting the right skateboard can make all the difference in your learning and skating experience. We hope the products we have reviewed and our buying guide help you choose the perfect skateboard that meets your expectations.
